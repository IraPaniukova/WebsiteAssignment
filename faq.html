If you have any question, try to find it here.

Q: When is it the latest I can order a cake before my event?

A: Hi. To be on the safe side, give it a month. But if it is not a busy
season (Xmas, Mother day, etc.), 2 weeks can be still fine. For the orders
that are less then 2 weeks before an additional fee can be applyed.


Q: What is the price of your cakes?

A: Hello. We calculate the price of you cake based on your order.
It depends on how big it is going to be and what type of the cake
you'd like to order. To calculate a approximate price you can on the
page "Contact" in the category "Order a cake". The price for mini
cakes is set. You can find it on the page "Mini cakes", under tab "Cakes".


Q: Can I customize the design and flavors of the cake?

A: Absolutely! We create custom cakes based on your preferences.
If you cannot find your favourite option in our standard sets, contact us
to discuss your preferences.


Q: What is your cancellation policy for cake orders?

A: Hello. We understand that plans can change, and we try to be as
flexible as possible. If you need to cancel your cake order, please
notify us as soon as possible. Depending on the circumstances and
the progress of the order, there may be a cancellation fee.


Q: Can I just pop up in your shop and buy a cake?

A: Hi. We have a range of cakes each morning which we sell in our caffee
The earlier you arrive the bigger chance to buy a whole cake you have.
Otherwise we can pack a range of different pieces for you.
